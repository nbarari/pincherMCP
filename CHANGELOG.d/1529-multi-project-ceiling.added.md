**Multi-project ceiling bench scaffolding (FILE-J, #1529).** `scripts/multi-project-ceiling-bench.sh` generates N synthetic projects (50 files each), indexes them, then times `pincher list --json` as a stable proxy for the watcher's per-tick disk-traversal cost. Records `{project_count, poll_cycle_ms_median}` per tier. `.github/workflows/multi-project-ceiling.yml` runs Friday 14:00 UTC + `workflow_dispatch`; default tiers are 10 + 50 on cron, 200 available via dispatch. **Phase 1 (v0.86-v0.90): advisory**; **Phase 2 (v0.91+): hard budget** per FILE-J acceptance — `poll_cycle_ms_median` exceeding published max will block release. Anchors the "recommended max concurrent indexed projects" budget rows in `docs/REFERENCE.md`. Methodology + proxy-vs-direct rationale in [docs/methodology/multi-project-ceiling.md](docs/methodology/multi-project-ceiling.md). Pairs with FILE-I (#1528) on the orthogonal axis (peak RSS at N files per project). Acceptance bullets #3 (auto-backoff above threshold) and #4 (cross-axis story) deferred to v0.87/v0.90 follow-ups.
