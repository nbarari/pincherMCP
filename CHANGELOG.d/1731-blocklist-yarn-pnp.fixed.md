The indexer now skips Yarn Plug'n'Play generated artifacts (#1731 v0.89). `.pnp.cjs` / `.pnp.js` (the PnP loader, often megabytes of generated code), `.pnp.loader.mjs` (the ESM loader), and `.pnp.data.json` (a lockfile-scale JSON data blob) were extracted as ordinary source — flooding the symbol store with loader-internal symbols (`$$SETUP_STATE`, `_interopNamespace`, error-code stubs) and producing `dead_code` false positives. These are never hand-written and are regenerated on every `yarn install`; `ast.ShouldSkip` now blocklists them by exact basename alongside lockfiles and minified bundles. Found dogfooding `dead_code` against a Yarn-PnP project.
